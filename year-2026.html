2026 Research Year

Second Street: The Stories of the Deuce

Telling the story of Jackson Ward through the rise, fall, and continued revitalization of its cultural main street.

For decades, Second Street was the cultural hub of Jackson Ward, the commercial and entertainment spine that gave the neighborhood its life and its reputation. The 2026 research takes a single street as its lens, arguing that the larger story of Jackson Ward, its flourishing, its decline, and its ongoing revival, can be read in the history of this one corridor.

The project traces Second Street across three chapters: its rise as a center of Black business and culture, its fall amid disinvestment and the disruptions of the twentieth century, and its continued revitalization as the community works to reclaim and reinterpret it today.
